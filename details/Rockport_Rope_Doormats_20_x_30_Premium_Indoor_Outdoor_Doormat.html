Rockport Rope Doormats

Add a touch of elegance and practicality to your home with these premium rope doormats. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, they are built to last with high-quality polypropylene rope that's soft underfoot yet highly durable.

Each mat is handwoven in small batches by skilled artisans in Vidor, TX, ensuring a unique, artisanal finish that feels like a piece of art. The 20" x 30" size is ideal for entryways, porches, decks, or even near the kitchen sink where moisture is common.

Thanks to their UV-stabilized material, these doormats retain their rich color and texture even when exposed to harsh sunlight. They are also easy to maintain with a quick rinse and tumble dry, or air dry for a hassle-free solution.

With a 50/50 brown and tan color palette and a brown insert, these doormats offer a warm, classic aesthetic that fits seamlessly into any decor. Whether you're welcoming guests in or keeping your outdoor space tidy, they combine style, comfort, and functionality in one elegant design.
